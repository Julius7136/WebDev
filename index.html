About Me

Hi, I’m Julius Salvacion, a 2nd year BSIT student soon stepping into my 3rd year. At first, I never thought I’d be in IT, my original plan was to take Criminology, with IT only as my second choice. But I changed my mind, and here I am.


As an IT student, I’ve faced many challenges, especially in programming since my first year. Poor time management often forced me to rush projects, even minor ones. I also struggled with distractions, spending too much time on online games instead of solving problems day by day.


Still, those experiences taught me something valuable: giving myself time to pause and think doesn’t weaken my mindset. I’ve seen classmates devote all their time to studying, sometimes at the cost of their health. I admire their dedication, but I also learned the importance of balance, caring for both mind and body.


This portfolio you’re viewing today was built in just one day if you add all the hours that i spent, but only because I had already planned the outline and design carefully, searching across different platforms for inspiration. It’s my very first portfolio, and I’m proud to share it with you.


Thank you for reading my story until the end.
