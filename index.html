About Me

I’m Julius, a BSIT student from Tuguegarao, Philippines, driven by a passion for creating practical and efficient software solutions. My focus is on desktop app development with VB.NET, where I specialize in dynamic UI workflows, database integration, and secure authentication systems.

I enjoy solving problems through clean code and user-friendly design, making sure every project balances functionality with simplicity. Alongside my technical work, I explore creative design tools like Photoshop and PicsArt, adding a visual edge to my projects.

My goal is to build a portfolio that reflects both my technical growth and my creative journey, showcasing projects that tell a story of learning, innovation, and continuous improvement.
